High-Protein Lemon Ice Cream

No Cook · High Protein · 2 servings · ~243 cal/serving

Ingredients

ItemAmountCalories
Full-fat cottage cheese2 cups~440
Lemon juice (fresh)3 tbsp~12
Lemon zest1 tbsp~5
Allulose3 tbsp~14
Vanilla extract1 tsp~12
Salt1/4 tsp0

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender or food processor (add cottage cheese last, liquids first).
  2. Blend 60–90 seconds until completely smooth and creamy.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness or lemon as needed.
  4. Pour into a loaf pan or freezer-safe container.
  5. Freeze 2–4 hours until firm (or overnight).
  6. Let sit 5 minutes before scooping.
  7. Top with extra zest if desired.

Nutrition (per serving)

~243
Calories
~25g
Protein

Tips

Full-fat cottage cheese = creamier texture.
If it's grainy, it's not blended enough.
Too hard? Blend again after freezing for soft-serve consistency.
Allulose stays scoopable frozen — no recrystallizing.
Boost protein: add 1 scoop vanilla protein powder.
Variation: fold in blueberries before freezing.
