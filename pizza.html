Air Fryer Cottage Cheese Pizza

Air Fryer · Low Carb · High Protein

Ingredients

ItemAmount
Cottage cheese (4%)1 cup
Large eggs2
Almond flour1/4 cup
Italian seasoning1 tsp
Everything bagel seasoning1 tbsp
Shredded parmesan4 oz (~1 cup)
Pizza sauce4.3 oz (~1/2 cup)
Pepperoni (optional)0.7 oz

Instructions

  1. Mix cottage cheese and eggs until smooth. Add almond flour, Italian seasoning, and everything bagel seasoning. Mix well.
  2. Spread evenly onto a parchment-lined 6–7″ air fryer pan.
  3. Air fry at 360°F for 24 minutes until golden and set.
  4. Add sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  5. Air fry another 3 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Cool slightly, slice, and enjoy.
