Banana Blueberry Air Fryer Bread

Air Fryer · Low Carb

Ingredients

ItemAmount
Ripe banana1 large
Egg1 large
Cottage cheese1/2 cup
Almond flour3/4 cup
Baking powder1/2 tsp
Saltpinch
Blueberries1/4 cup
Vanilla extract1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Mash banana in a bowl.
  2. Add egg and cottage cheese, mix until smooth.
  3. Stir in almond flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Fold in blueberries.
  5. Pour into a greased ramekin or small loaf pan.
  6. Air fry at 360°F for 24 minutes.
  7. Cool 10 minutes before removing.

Notes

Variation: use 3 bananas, no berries, 2 eggs, and cook at 360°F for 26 minutes.
