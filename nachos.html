Chicharrón Nachos

Oven · Low Carb · High Protein

Ingredients

ItemAmount
Chicharrones1 bag
Lean ground beef1 lb
Shredded aged cheddar cheese2 cups
Onion, diced1/2
Salt1 tsp
Cumin1/2 tsp
Pepper1/4 tsp
Cilantro peppertiniesto taste
Sour creamto taste
Salsato taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté ground beef with diced onion until cooked through.
  2. Season with salt, cumin, and pepper.
  3. Spread chicharrones on a flat baking tray and top with shredded cheddar cheese.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 5 minutes until cheese is melted.
  5. Layer seasoned beef over the cheesy chicharrones.
  6. Top with cilantro peppertinies, salsa, and sour cream.
