# No-Bake Reese's Balls (SF / Low Sugar)
Makes 12 balls

---

## Ingredients

| Ingredient                        | Amount       | Calories | Protein | Net Carbs | Fat  |
|-----------------------------------|--------------|----------|---------|-----------|------|
| Peanut butter powder (PB Fit)     | 1 cup (92g)  | 340      | 34g     | 20g       | 10g  |
| No-sugar-added applesauce         | ½ cup        | 50       | 0g      | 13g       | 0g   |
| SF chocolate chips — Bake Believe | ½ cup (85g)  | 140      | 2g      | 6g*       | 12g  |
| Allulose powder                   | 1 Tbl (~12g) | 5        | 0g      | 0g†       | 0g   |
| Flaky sea salt                    | pinch/ball   | 0        | 0g      | 0g        | 0g   |

*Net carbs after subtracting erythritol and fiber  
†Allulose is not metabolized — counts as 0 net carbs

---

## Steps

1. **Mix the dough** — Combine PB powder, applesauce, and allulose in a bowl and stir until a thick, scoopable dough forms.
2. **Roll the balls** — Divide into 12 equal portions and roll each into a smooth ball. Place on a parchment-lined plate.
3. **Freeze to firm** — Freeze balls for 15 minutes so they hold up during dipping.
4. **Melt chocolate** — Set a heatproof bowl over a saucepan with 2 inches of barely simmering water (do not let bowl touch water). Add Bake Believe chips and stir continuously with a spatula until fully melted and smooth. Remove from heat.
5. **Dip and coat** — Roll each ball in melted chocolate using a fork or toothpick, letting excess drip off. Place back on parchment.
6. **Salt and set** — Sprinkle flaky sea salt on each ball immediately, then refrigerate 20–30 minutes until chocolate is firm.

> **Note:** Bake Believe chips scorch fast in the microwave — double boiler only.
> Stir constantly and pull off heat as soon as smooth. Residual heat finishes the job.
