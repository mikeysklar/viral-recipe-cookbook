Banana Blueberry Air Fryer Bread

Ingredients

| Item           | Amount  |
| -------------- | ------- |
| Ripe banana    | 1 large |
| Egg            | 1 large |
| Cottage cheese | 1/2 cup |
| Almond flour   | 3/4 cup |
| Baking powder  | 1/2 tsp |
| Salt           | pinch   |
| Blueberries    | 1/4 cup |
| vanilla        | 1 tsp   |

Instructions
1. Mash banana in bowl
2. Add egg + cottage cheese, mix smooth
3. Stir in almond flour, baking powder, salt
4. Fold in blueberries
5. Pour into greased ramekin or small loaf pan

Air Fry @ 360°F for 24 min
Cool 10 min before removing
