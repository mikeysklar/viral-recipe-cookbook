Zucchini Asiago Everything Bagel Wrap

Air Fryer · Low Carb

Ingredients

ItemAmount
Zucchini (medium, grated & squeezed dry)1
Eggs2
Shredded Asiago cheese1/2 cup
Psyllium husk powder2 tbsp
Everything bagel seasoning2 tbsp + extra for topping

Instructions

  1. Grate zucchini and squeeze aggressively in a towel — repeat twice. Moisture is the enemy of a wrap that holds together.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until a thick, spreadable batter forms.
  3. Line air fryer basket with parchment paper.
  4. Spread batter as thin as possible — aim for 1/8″ or less. Use the back of a spoon or offset spatula.
  5. Sprinkle extra everything bagel seasoning generously over the top.
  6. Air fry at 375°F for 10–13 minutes until golden and edges lift.
  7. Flip carefully, cook another 3–4 minutes until dry to the touch.
  8. Cool 5 minutes on a rack — it firms up as it cools.
  9. Fill and roll like a wrap, or fold like a flatbread.

~220

Tips

Everything bagel seasoning already has salt and garlic — no need to add more.
Thinner = more flexible. Thicker = more bread-like, less pliable.
Psyllium husk is key — don't skip it or sub with flax (too wet).
Pairs perfectly with cream cheese, lox, or turkey and avocado fillings.
Make a batch and refrigerate between parchment sheets for up to 4 days.
