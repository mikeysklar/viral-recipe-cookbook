Air Fryer Frittata

Air Fryer · Low Carb · High Protein

Ingredients

ItemAmount
Eggs6 large
Broccoli1 cup chopped, blanched 2 min, dried
Chicken sausagediced
Cherry tomatoes1 dozen, sliced
Shaved Asiago cheese1/4 cup shredded
Everything bagel seasoning1 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Line air fryer pan with a parchment paper square.
  2. Blend eggs with everything bagel seasoning.
  3. Place broccoli, chicken sausage, and most of the cheese in the pan.
  4. Pour egg mixture over the add-ins. Top with remaining cheese.
  5. Air fry at 350°F for 20 minutes.
  6. Lift tray and place on a rack.
  7. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.
