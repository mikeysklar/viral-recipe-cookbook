# 🍋 High-Protein Lemon Ice Cream
### Viral Cottage Cheese Ice Cream Method

**Yield:** 2 servings | **Protein:** ~25g | **Prep:** 5 min | **Freeze:** 2–4 hrs
**Total Calories:** ~485 kcal | **Per Serving:** ~243 kcal

---

## Ingredients

add cottage cheese last, liquid first

| #   | Item                    | Amount | Calories |
| --- | ----------------------- | ------ | -------- |
| 1   | Full-fat cottage cheese | 2 cups | ~440 cal |
| 2   | Lemon juice (fresh)     | 3 tbsp | ~12 cal  |
| 3   | Lemon zest              | 1 tbsp | ~5 cal   |
| 4   | Allulose                | 3 tbsp | ~14 cal  |
| 5   | Vanilla extract         | 1 tsp  | ~12 cal  |
| 6   | Salt                    | ¼ tsp  | 0 cal    |
|     | **Total**               |        | **~483** |

---

## Steps

| Step | Action                                              |
|------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| 1    | Add all ingredients to a blender or food processor  |
| 2    | Blend 60–90 sec until completely smooth and creamy  |
| 3    | Taste and adjust sweetness or lemon as needed       |
| 4    | Pour into a loaf pan or freezer-safe container      |
| 5    | Freeze 2–4 hrs until firm (or overnight)            |
| 6    | Let sit 5 min before scooping                       |
| 7    | Scoop and top with extra zest if desired            |

---

## Tips

| Tip               | Detail                                     |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| 🥛 Cottage cheese | Full-fat = creamier texture                |
| 🌀 Blend well     | Grainy = not blended enough                |
| 🍦 Too hard?      | Blend again after freezing for soft-serve  |
| 🍬 Allulose perk  | Stays scoopable frozen, no recrystallizing |
| ➕ Boost it        | Add 1 scoop vanilla protein powder         |
| 🍓 Variation      | Fold in blueberries before freezing        |
