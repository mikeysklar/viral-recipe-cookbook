No-Bake Peanut Chocolate Salty Balls

No Bake · Sugar Free · Makes 12 balls

Ingredients

ItemAmountCaloriesProteinNet CarbsFat
Peanut butter powder (PB Fit)1 cup (92g)34034g20g10g
No-sugar-added applesauce1/2 cup500g13g0g
SF chocolate chips (Bake Believe)1/2 cup (85g)1402g6g12g
Allulose powder1 tbsp (~12g)50g0g0g
Flaky sea saltpinch per ball00g0g0g

Instructions

  1. Mix the dough — Combine PB powder, applesauce, and allulose in a bowl and stir until a thick, scoopable dough forms.
  2. Roll the balls — Divide into 12 equal portions and roll each into a smooth ball. Place on a parchment-lined plate.
  3. Freeze to firm — Freeze balls for 15 minutes so they hold up during dipping.
  4. Melt chocolate — Set a heatproof bowl over a saucepan with 2 inches of barely simmering water (don't let the bowl touch water). Add chips and stir continuously until melted and smooth. Remove from heat.
  5. Dip and coat — Roll each ball in melted chocolate using a fork or toothpick, letting excess drip off. Place back on parchment.
  6. Salt and set — Sprinkle flaky sea salt on each ball immediately, then refrigerate 20–30 minutes until chocolate is firm.
