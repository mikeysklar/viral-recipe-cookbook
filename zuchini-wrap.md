# Low-Carb Zucchini Asiago Everything Bagel Sandwich Wrap

## Ingredients
- 1 medium zucchini, grated and squeezed bone dry
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup shredded Asiago cheese
- 2 tbsp psyllium husk powder (binds + adds flexibility)
- 2 tbsp everything bagel seasoning (plus extra for topping)

## Instructions

1. Grate zucchini and squeeze aggressively in a towel — repeat twice.
   Moisture is the enemy of a wrap that holds together.
2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until a thick, spreadable batter forms.
3. Line air fryer basket with parchment paper.
4. Spread batter as THIN as possible — aim for 1/8 inch or less.
   Use the back of a spoon or offset spatula.
5. Sprinkle extra everything bagel seasoning generously over the top.
6. Air fry at 375°F (190°C) for 10–13 minutes until golden and edges lift.
7. Flip carefully, cook another 3–4 minutes until dry to the touch.
8. Let cool 5 minutes on a rack — it firms up as it cools.
9. Fill and roll like a wrap, or fold like a flatbread.

## Macros (approximate, 1 wrap)
- Calories: ~220
- Carbs: ~5g net
- Protein: ~16g
- Fat: ~14g

## Tips
- Everything bagel seasoning already has salt and garlic — no need to add more.
- Thinner = more flexible. Thicker = more bread-like, less pliable.
- Psyllium husk is key — don't skip it or sub with flax (too wet).
- Pairs perfectly with cream cheese, lox, or turkey and avocado fillings.
- Make a batch and refrigerate between parchment sheets for up to 4 days.
