## 🍕 Air Fryer Cottage Cheese Pizza

### Ingredients
| Ingredient           | Amount          |
| -------------------- | --------------- |
| Cottage cheese (4%)  | 1 cup           |
| Large eggs           | 2               |
| Almond flour         | 1/4 cup         |
| Italian seasoning    | 1 tsp           |
| everything bagel     | 1 Tbl           |
| Shredded parmesan    | 4 oz (~1 cup)   |
| Pizza sauce          | 4.3 oz (~½ cup) |
| Pepperoni (optional) | 0.7 oz          |
### Method (Air Fryer)
| Step | Instructions                                                                                         |
| ---- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | Mix cottage cheese and eggs until smooth. Add flour, Italian seasoning, and garlic powder. Mix well. |
| 2    | Spread evenly onto a parchment-lined 6–7" air fryer pan.                                             |
| 3    | Air fry at 360°F for 24 minutes until golden and set.                                                |
| 4    | Add sauce, cheese, and toppings.                                                                     |
| 5    | Air fry another 3 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.                                         |
| 6    | Cool slightly, slice, and enjoy.                                                                     |
