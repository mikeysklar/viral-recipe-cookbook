Low-Carb Air Fryer Flatbread

Air Fryer · Low Carb · Parmesan Top

Ingredients

ItemAmountNotes
Eggs2 largeblend
Cottage cheese1 cupblend
Almond flour1 cupmix in
Everything bagel seasoning1 tspmix in
Parmesan2–3 tbspsprinkle on top

Instructions

  1. Blend eggs and cottage cheese until smooth.
  2. Mix in almond flour and seasoning. Let rest 2–3 minutes.
  3. Spread about 1/4″ thick on parchment paper.
  4. Shake parmesan on top.
  5. Air fry at 350°F for ~20 minutes until edges are brown and center is set.
  6. Cool 5 minutes in the tray (open).
  7. Flip onto a rack and dry 10 minutes.
